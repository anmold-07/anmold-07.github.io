Welcome to the Digital Notebook

What is Digital Notebook about?

This blog is a place for me to share my views on a number of topics that interest me. It mainly focuses on science, technology, engineering, mathematics and some thoughts on life in general.

Why I am doing this?

With an overabundance of content that is available online, finding useful references that facilitate understanding of highly technical content is a sort of a hit and trial process; to know if it is useful you have to spend time going through the material. As a consequence, you spend a lot of time \(\textit{finding}\) useful content than actually reading about it (I find my self doing that when I don't understand an intricate concept). I find this to be a very time-draining process and as a student/practitioner, spending too much time finding such references is also not an viable option. Let's take a simple Linear Algebra course for example. While there exists many phenomenal textbooks on this subject, there are still many concepts that people don't completely understand. However, thanks to one of such beautiful reference, intricate concepts of linear algebra are now easily understood by many. Unfortunately, there exist many such blogs/videos/playlists on the internet but it's just that people are not aware of them. This blog is an attempt to bridge that gap.
